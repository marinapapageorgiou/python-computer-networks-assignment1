Between the available universities in the list from the bilateral agreements the USI stood out. I was going to apply at 2020 but because of the covid situation I thought it would be better to wait for a year. And so that happened. I applied last year. I got accepted and then I applied for to stay at USIHome. And that is where I live for now. Also, I thought it was a good opprtunity to get a glimpse of Switzerland. Lugano is located by the Alps, which is a great opportunity to exploit to visit the beautiful places and landscapes of the Alps.
